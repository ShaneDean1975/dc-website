Compensation Available in a Pensacola Car Accident Claim

Under Florida law, injured victims may pursue compensation for:

  • Emergency medical treatment
  • Hospital bills and surgeries
  • Rehabilitation and therapy
  • Lost income and reduced earning capacity
  • Pain and suffering
  • Mental anguish
  • Permanent disability
  • Property damage and diminished vehicle value

Serious injury cases may allow you to step outside Florida’s no-fault system and pursue full damages against the at-fault driver.

Florida Car Accident Laws You Should Know

Florida operates under a no-fault insurance system requiring Personal Injury Protection (PIP) coverage. However, if your injuries meet Florida’s serious injury threshold, you can file a liability claim against the negligent driver.

The statute of limitations for most personal injury claims in Florida is two years from the date of the accident. Delaying action can significantly weaken your case.

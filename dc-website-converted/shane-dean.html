About Shane Dean

Shane Dean is a highly respected personal injury attorney with over 20 years of experience representing accident victims in Florida and Alabama. Throughout his career, he has successfully recovered tens of millions of dollars for clients injured in car accidents, truck accidents, motorcycle collisions, and wrongful death cases.

Mr. Dean's exceptional legal skills and dedication to his clients have earned him recognition as a Florida Super Lawyers Rising Stars for three consecutive years, an honor bestowed upon only 2.5% of attorneys in the state. He is also a proud member of the Multi-Million Dollar Advocates Forum, a prestigious organization limited to trial lawyers who have won multi-million dollar verdicts and settlements.

Shane's approach to personal injury law is built on thorough case preparation, aggressive representation, and genuine compassion for those he serves. He understands that every case involves real people facing real challenges, and he treats each client with the respect and attention they deserve. His commitment to excellence has resulted in numerous substantial settlements and verdicts that have helped accident victims rebuild their lives.

Known for his courtroom prowess and negotiation skills, Shane prepares every case for trial from day one. This trial-ready approach sends a clear message to insurance companies that he is willing to fight for maximum compensation, often resulting in favorable settlements without the need for lengthy litigation. However, when a fair settlement cannot be reached, Shane is an experienced and effective trial attorney who is not afraid to take cases to court.

About Chad Camper

Chad Camper is a dedicated personal injury attorney who has successfully recovered millions of dollars for his clients throughout Florida and Alabama. As a member of the Million Dollar Advocates Forum, an exclusive organization limited to fewer than 1% of U.S. lawyers, Mr. Camper has demonstrated his exceptional ability to secure substantial compensation for accident victims.

Before pursuing his legal career, Chad proudly served five years in the U.S. Air Force, where he received the prestigious John L. Levitow Award, the highest honor awarded to enlisted personnel in professional military education. This military background instilled in him a strong sense of duty, discipline, and commitment to serving others - values that he brings to every case he handles.

Mr. Camper earned his Juris Doctor from West Virginia University College of Law and is licensed to practice in both Florida and West Virginia. He specializes in personal injury cases including car accidents, truck accidents, motorcycle accidents, wrongful death claims, and complex insurance disputes. His approach combines thorough case preparation, aggressive advocacy, and compassionate client service.

Known for his trial-ready approach, Chad prepares every case as if it will go to trial, which often results in better settlement offers from insurance companies. He understands that each case is unique and requires personalized attention, and he ensures that every client receives the individualized service they deserve during what is often the most difficult time in their lives.
